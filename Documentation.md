**Introduction**

BMI is Short for Body Mass Index. It is computed  as a composite of of Weight and Height. 
I is a good predictor of health issues and may be used for both children and adults

**Algorithm**

The algorithm  has two inputs - the height in inches and the weight in pounds. 

The BMI formula is

*BMI = Weight*703/Height^2


**Program Implementation**

The program was implemented as an R Shiny App and consisted of two parts server.R and ui.R. The  ui.R implemented the front end and server.R implmented the back-end


**How to Use**

Input the height and weight. The program computes the BMI automatically
